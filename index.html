Covid Vaccine

A vaccine is intended to provide immunity against COVID-19. In general, vaccines contain weakened or inactive parts of a particular organism that triggers an immune response within the body. This weakened version will not cause the disease in the person receiving the vaccine, but it will prompt their immune system to respond.

Covid PCR Test

A Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) test is the only type accepted to travel to South Africa. This type of test detects whether an individual is infected with coronavirus at the time it was taken. Samples taken during PCR tests need to be sent to a laboratory for processing which means results are not usually available until the following day.
